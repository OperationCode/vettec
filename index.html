What It Is:

The Vet Tec bill, is a bill that will be enacted to direct the Secretary of veterans affairs to carry out a technology education pilot program, making code schools more accesssible for veterans by providing eligible veterans with the opportunity to enroll in high technology programs of education.

What It Does:

The Vet Tec bill provides funding for high technology training, such as bootcamps, as well as a housing stipend for veterans who wish to pursue a career in technology.

Eligibility:

The Vet Tec bill would cover any veteran who is entitled to education assistance administered by the VA.

What It Means:

The Vet Tec bill, will allow the ability for veterans who wish to pursue a career in technology to get the education that they need, with not only the support of the department of Veterans Affairs, but the education institutions in which they would be attending.

Who Benefits:

Our Veterans: Passing this bill will ensure that our veterans will have access to high quality education and to better jobs, guaranteeing a better quality of life.

Their Families: Passing this bill will ensure that our veterans will be able to re-enter the workforce in a timely fashion, thus making transition from military life to civilian life a lot less hectic. It will lessen the strain that some military families feel while making adjustmets to civilian life.

The Tech Community: The passing of this bill will not only enable veterans to improve their lives, but also veterans will be able to put that discipline, and hard work to use outside of the military. By aiding our vets in entering the modern work force we are tapping into a veritable pool of untapped talent that would otherwise go to waste because programs like this just does not exist.
